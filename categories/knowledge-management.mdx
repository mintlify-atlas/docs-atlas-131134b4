Knowledge management is the collection of methods relating to creating, sharing, using and managing the knowledge and information.

## Applications

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="AFFiNE Community Edition" icon="infinity" href="https://affine.pro/">
    Next-gen knowledge base that brings planning, sorting and creating all together. Privacy first, customizable and ready to use (alternative to Notion and Miro).
    
    **License:** MIT/AGPL-3.0 | **Platform:** Docker
  </Card>

  <Card title="Atomic Server" icon="atom" href="https://github.com/atomicdata-dev/atomic-server">
    Knowledge graph database with documents (similar to Notion), tables, search, and a powerful linked data API. Lightweight, very fast and no runtime dependencies.
    
    **License:** MIT | **Platform:** Docker/Rust
  </Card>

  <Card title="Digimindmap" icon="diagram-project" href="https://ladigitale.dev/digimindmap/#/">
    Create simple mindmaps (documentation in French).
    
    **License:** AGPL-3.0 | **Platform:** Nodejs/PHP
  </Card>

  <Card title="LibreKB" icon="book" href="https://librekb.com/">
    Web-based knowledge base solution. A simple web app, it runs on pretty much any web server or hosting provider with PHP and MySQL.
    
    **License:** GPL-3.0 | **Platform:** PHP
  </Card>

  <Card title="memEx" icon="memory" href="https://gitea.bubbletea.dev/shibao/memEx">
    Structured personal knowledge base, inspired by zettlekasten and org-mode.
    
    **License:** AGPL-3.0 | **Platform:** Docker
  </Card>

  <Card title="SiYuan" icon="s" href="https://b3log.org/siyuan/">
    A privacy-first personal knowledge management software, written in typescript and golang.
    
    **License:** AGPL-3.0 | **Platform:** Docker/Go
  </Card>

  <Card title="TeamMapper" icon="users" href="https://github.com/b310-digital/teammapper">
    Host and create your own mindmaps. Share your mindmap sessions with your team and collaborate live on mindmaps.
    
    **License:** MIT | **Platform:** Docker/Nodejs
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
