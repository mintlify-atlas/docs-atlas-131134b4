[Webmail](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Webmail) clients.

## Modern Webmail Clients

### SnappyMail

Simple, modern, lightweight & fast web-based email client (fork of RainLoop).

- **Demo:** [snappymail.eu/demo](https://snappymail.eu/demo/)
- **Source Code:** [GitHub](https://github.com/the-djmaze/snappymail)
- **Plugins:** [snappymail.eu/repository](https://snappymail.eu/repository/v2/plugins/)
- **License:** AGPL-3.0
- **Tech Stack:** PHP

### Roundcube

Browser-based IMAP client with an application-like user interface.

- **Source Code:** [GitHub](https://github.com/roundcube/roundcubemail)
- **License:** GPL-3.0
- **Tech Stack:** PHP, deb

### Cypht

Feed reader for your email accounts.

- **Source Code:** [GitHub](https://github.com/cypht-org/cypht)
- **License:** LGPL-2.1
- **Tech Stack:** PHP

## Classic Webmail
