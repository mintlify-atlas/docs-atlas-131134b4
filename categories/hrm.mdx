A [human resources management system](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Human_resource_management_system) combines a number of systems and processes to ensure the easy management of [human resources](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Human_resources), business processes and data.

## Applications

### admidio

User management system for websites of organizations and groups. The system has a flexible role model so that it's possible to reflect the structure and permissions of your organization.

- **Website**: [admidio.org](https://www.admidio.org/)
- **Demo**: [admidio.org/demo](https://www.admidio.org/demo/)
- **Source Code**: [github.com/Admidio/admidio](https://github.com/Admidio/admidio)
- **License**: GPL-2.0
- **Tech Stack**: PHP, Docker

### Frappe HR

Complete HRMS solution with over 13 different modules right from employee management, onboarding, leaves, to payroll, taxation, and more.

- **Website**: [frappe.io/hr](https://frappe.io/hr)
- **Source Code**: [github.com/frappe/hrms](https://github.com/frappe/hrms)
- **License**: GPL-3.0
- **Tech Stack**: Docker, Python, Nodejs

### MintHCM

Tool for Human Capital Management based on two popular, well-known business applications SugarCRM Community Edition and SuiteCRM.

- **Website**: [minthcm.org](https://minthcm.org/)
- **Source Code**: [github.com/minthcm/minthcm](https://github.com/minthcm/minthcm)
- **License**: AGPL-3.0
- **Tech Stack**: PHP
