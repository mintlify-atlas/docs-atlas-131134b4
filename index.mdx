## Welcome to Awesome Selfhosted

Self-hosting is the practice of hosting and managing applications on your own server(s) instead of consuming from [SaaSS](https://www.gnu.org/philosophy/who-does-that-server-really-serve.html) providers.

This is a comprehensive catalog of **Free Software** network services and web applications which can be hosted on your own servers. Our community has curated over **1,000 applications** across **80+ categories**, making it the largest and most complete resource for self-hosted software.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Category">
    Browse the navigation menu to find applications in your area of interest
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Applications">
    Each application listing includes a description, demo links, source code, license information, and technology stack
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy Your Software">
    Follow the project's documentation to deploy and configure the application on your own server
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contribute Back">
    Share your experience and help others by contributing to this list
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  This list focuses on **Free Software** (as defined by the Free Software Foundation). Non-free software with restrictions is listed separately in the [Non-Free Software](/non-free/overview) section.
</Note>
